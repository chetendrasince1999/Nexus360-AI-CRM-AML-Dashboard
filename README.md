## Overview

Nexus360 is an enterprise-style SaaS platform built to demonstrate CRM Analytics, AML Monitoring, Customer Intelligence, Executive Reporting, AI Copilot Workflows and Risk Management.

The platform simulates how modern organizations monitor customer health, revenue exposure, compliance risks and executive KPIs.

---

## Key Features

- Executive War Room
- CRM Analytics Dashboard
- AML Monitoring Center
- AI Risk Copilot
- Customer 360 View
- Scenario Simulator
- Boardroom Reporting
- Executive Intelligence Dashboard
- Portfolio Scoring Engine
- AI Chat Assistant
- Animated Enterprise UI
- Cloud Hosted Deployment
